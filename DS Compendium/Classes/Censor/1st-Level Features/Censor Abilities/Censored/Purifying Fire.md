# Purifying Fire (5 Wrath)

| :------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------- |
| **Keywords:** Magic, Melee, Ranged, Strike, Weapon | **Type:** Action         |
| **Distance:** Melee 1 or ranged 5                  | **Target:** One creature |
